What is WARJORN?

Step into the realm of WARJORN, an expansive Minecraft political network that transcends conventional gaming experiences. Immerse yourself in a world where politics intertwine with gameplay, offering a diverse array of engaging gamemodes shaped by the dynamics of governance and diplomacy. Here, the canvas is yours to paint: carve your unique path through history, craft alliances, or forge your destiny as a solo trailblazer.

How to join

Here you can see, how you can join on the WARJORN server.

1

Add the server

Go to Multiplayer in Minecraft, then Add Server
2

Enter server adress

Enter "play.warjorn.xyz:2247" in the server address field and click "Done".
3

You're done!

Now you can play on WARJORN with other players!
